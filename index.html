BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment

Achievements

Awards

First Asian act in AMA history to win Artist of the Year and the first Korean pop act to receive a Grammy Award nomination

Guiness World Records

BTS have attained 23 Guinness World Records, including for the most Twitter engagements and for the most viewed video/music video on YouTube in 24 hours
